Pig Game:

There are basically 2 players, and the player who gets the 20 scores at first, wins it.

Buttons:

Roll Dice: This button Rolls the dice and the number of dice becomes the current score of the player, and every time the player rolls the dice, the number gets added and the total sum is displayed below the player number who is playing.

Hold: This button holds the score of the current player and the other player gets the turn to roll the dice.

New Game: The game gets in restart mode and all the value becomes zero.

Rules:

- If a player gets 1 on the dice, then the turn gets automatically shifted to another player and the current score of the player becomes zero.

- The player who successfully gets the total score of 20 or above, wins and the color of the player side gets changed to black.
